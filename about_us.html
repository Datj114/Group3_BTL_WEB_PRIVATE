MONA FURNITURE MANUFACTURER

MONA MEDIA Co., Ltd is an Outdoor Furniture Manufacturer, specializing in the products of poly rattan wicker hand-woven over metal frame such as sofa, chairs, coffee sets, dining sets, bistro sets, sunbeds, planters, etc.

Established in 2014, our factory is located in Bien Hoa City, Dong Nai province of Vietnam, which is about 1.5h driving from Ho Chi Minh City airport or downtown.

Possessing skilled craftmen, well-qualified mechanical workers, dedicated office people and passionate leaders, we effort to respond our partners with competency, sincerity and dedication, looking for mutual co-prosperity.
